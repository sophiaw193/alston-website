ParkMe: Parking Made Easy

This project addresses the parking problem of Cornell students who live in Collegetown. Although many students own a vehicle, street parking may be hard to find and parking spaces can be expensive. The goal of this project is to improve the experience of finding overnight parking, Collegetown parking, and on-campus parking for Cornell students.
